What is your VAT number?

The registered VAT number for Trainer Bubble Ltd. is GB 161 2412 50. This number will appear on all sales receipts and invoices that we issue.

Why do you charge VAT on some products, but not others?

As of 1st May 2020 the HMRC brought in legislation to make e-publications zero rated. This zero rated status applies to our training course materials, which are considered e-publications and are supplied electronically. This same status does not apply to e-learning content or videos, which are not considered ‘e-publications’. This leaves us with a peculiar situation, whereby we apply VAT to some of our products, but others are zero rated. We have taken care of this calculation on our website and applied VAT according to legislation, so you should be able to identify clearly when you are paying VAT or not.

Overseas clients should not be charged VAT at all and this calculation is also taken care of by our website.

How do I download your free training resources?

This is how to download our free resources…

  1. Click on the ‘Free Training Resources’ menu bar on the left of any page.
  2. Select the section that you want a free training resource in i.e. energisers, training games, icebreakers etc.
  3. You will be presented with a collection of images with the free resource title listed underneath. Select the one you want.
  4. You will now be viewing the synopsis of the specific free training resource.
  5. At the bottom of the page is a ‘Download Now’ button. Click this and the resource will open in the relevant format.
  6. Save the training game, energiser, icebreaker or resource to your computer, edit and alter to your needs and print off to use.
  7. Sign Up to our newsletter and be informed when we add new energisers, icebreakers, training games, and training resources.
